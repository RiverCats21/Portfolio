Professional Overview

I am a construction management and facilities engineering professional with a strong foundation in civil engineering principles and a growing focus on how artificial intelligence and data analytics can improve decision‑making across the built environment. My academic and professional experiences span cost estimating, scheduling, facilities planning, and infrastructure support, with an emphasis on practical application rather than theoretical experimentation.

Through my coursework and projects, I have explored how AI‑enabled tools can enhance risk analysis, optimize lifecycle costs, and support more reliable project delivery. I am particularly interested in bridging traditional construction practices with emerging digital technologies to create measurable improvements in productivity and asset performance.

Education

  • M.S. Construction & Facilities Engineering – University of North Carolina at Charlotte
  • B.S. Construction Management – University of North Carolina at Charlotte
  • Civil Engineering Technology Diploma – Fayetteville Technical Community College

Technical Focus

  • AI‑assisted decision support in construction and facilities management
  • Cost estimating, scheduling, and project controls
  • Facilities lifecycle planning and predictive maintenance concepts
  • Building information modeling (Revit) and construction visualization
  • Data analysis using spreadsheets and structured decision models

Professional Experience Highlights

My experience includes roles in both public infrastructure and private construction services. I have supported roadway and permitting operations with the North Carolina Department of Transportation, assisted in project estimating and scheduling for commercial and residential work, and completed academic projects focused on facilities cost estimation and BIM‑based scheduling.

These experiences have shaped my interest in using structured analysis and emerging technologies to support better planning, coordination, and execution throughout the project lifecycle.
